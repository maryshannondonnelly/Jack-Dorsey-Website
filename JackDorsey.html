Jack Dorsey History

Jack Dorsey was born and raised in St. Louis, Missouri. He grew up going to a catholic high school and found intrest in dispatch routing. With dispatch routing he could track where the police cars and ambulances were going around the city. Later in his life he moved to New York City and attended NYU but eventually dropped out. At NYU he found his love for coding and computers.



















Inspiration

  • Twitter was inspired by the emergency dispatch center in St. Louis. He was at home a lot, where he would play on his computer and listen to the police scanner. He was fascinated by the short bursts of talking used by law enforcement which was his inspiration for the social networking.

Twitter and Square Start Up

  • Twitter

At NYU Dorsey came up with the idea of Twitter along with a friend of his Noah Glass. The main idea was just to update your friends and family using SMS among multiple carriers. The 140 character limit was due to the 160 SMS character limit plus the 20 extra character limit for your username. The name twitter came from the words "twitch" and "jitter," while the term tweet came from the users.

  • Square

    • The idea of Square came about when one of Dorsey's close friends complained to him about losing thousands of dollars and tons of customers to the fact that he could not accept credit cards. The device connects to the phone's audio jack and all you have to do is slide your credit card and the details would go onto the phone. The device can use magnetic strip and strip cards. This device is free, however the company gets a certain percentage per transaction.
